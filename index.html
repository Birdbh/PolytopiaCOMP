Tournament Specifics

Find all the information about the tournament below.

Who

All residence can particpiate in the tournament. All you need is a phone and a passion to win hundreds of dollars.

What

The tournament is a mobile Polytopia tournament. The first place winner gets $400 in cash! The tournament will also have free food and beverages for residence to meet each other and compete while staying energized.

When

The tournament will be held at 1:00 p.m. on Sunday Septemeber 16th. The tournament matches will start 2:00 p.m. Please arrive before 1:30 p.m. to verify you have made a Champions Account, have Polytopia Downloaded, and have registered for the tournament.

Where

The event will be held in the Nenchako Gathering Room above the Pritchard Dinning Hall

Why

Why not! You get to win cash, play games, and get free food.

How

Follow the simple instructions of the website. Download the app and learn the mechanics via the tutorial. Come to the option practice session. Register of Champions and sign up for the tournament. Finnaly, show up on the day of the tournament to have a chance at winning over $700 of prizes.
