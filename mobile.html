Mobile Application

Polytopia is played both on Android and Iphone devices. The links at the bottom of the page can be used to access the application in the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Polytopia is free to play and requires no purchases to participate in the tournament and win the cash prizes! It is required to download and highly reccomended become familar with the app before the tournament date. Click Here to learn more about our pre-tournament practice session and becoming familar with the game.

Polytopia can also be played on laptops via purchase in the Steam App Store. This is not required and not reccomended but players may choose to use this platform if they desire.
