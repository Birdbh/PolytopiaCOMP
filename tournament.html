Tournament Information

To play in the tournament players must sign up using the Google Form Sign Up Link and show up on time for the tournament. All cash prizes will be distributed immediatly following the completion of the tournament. Food and snacks will be provided for recharging and socializing.

Tournament Rules:

  1. All players must be present at the time of the tournament start time
  2. All players must have a Challengermode Tournament Account and a Polytopia Account Connected
    • A Challengermode account can be created and connected to Polytopia for Free
  3. All players must join the tournament before the start time
    • Directions for how to join and play in the tournament an be found here
  4. All players must be residence at the time of the tournament
  5. Any occurence of cheating or unsporting like conduct will mean immediate disqualification from the tournamet
  6. The tournament will run in a Single Elimination fashion
  7. Matches will be 1v1 on a tiny map
  8. Only free to play tribes are allowed, this means Luxidoor, Vengir, Quetzali, etc are banned from play
  9. To recieve your prize in cash you must be 18 years or older, those under 18 will recieve their prize as a gift card to wherever they want
  10. Players must agree to a photo release form for media taken only during the tournament for release to Midjiwan AB, Polytopias parent company
