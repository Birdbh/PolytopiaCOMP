Tournament Information

To play in the tournament, players must fill in the Google Form Sign Up and show up on time for the tournament. All cash prizes will be distributed immediately following the completion of the tournament. Food and snacks will be provided for recharging and socializing.

Tournament Rules:

  1. All players must be present at the beginning of the tournament start time
  2. All players must have a Challengermode Tournament Account connected to a Polytopia Account
    • A Challengermode account can be created and connected to Polytopia for Free
  3. All players must sign up before the day of the tournament
    • Directions for how to join and play in the tournament an be found here
  4. All players must be residents at the time of the tournament
  5. Any instances of cheating or unsporting-like conduct will result in immediate disqualification from the tournament
  6. The tournament will run in a Single Elimination format
  7. Matches will be 1 vs 1 on a tiny map
  8. Only tribes that are free to play are permitted; for example, Luxidoor, Vengir, Quetzali, etc, are banned from play
  9. To receive the cash prize, participants must be 18 years or older. Participants under 18 will receive their prize as a gift card to the store of their choice
  10. Players must consent to a photo release form, granting permission to Midjiwan AB, the parent company of Polytopia, to use media collected during the tournament for distribution and marketing
